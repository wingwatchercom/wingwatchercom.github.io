    Here is a list of resource for you if you want to visit the northwest corner of the state. That's my area and I will be here to show you the birds we have. I will take you along one of Connecticut's famous rivers, the Farmington River. Anglers from around the world come to Trout fish in this beautiful river. I have many private birding places where few people go and I will share them with you. There are some great views of the valleys and hills that make up the contour of my area and this diverse landscape brings with it a good variety of bird species. Not only that, we have other creatures that you may enjoy seeing like the Black Bear, Red Fox, White-tailed Deer, and the Beaver. Of course, I don't just bird in the northwest corner. I could take you to other environs on a moments notice. Connecticut is also blessed with an excellent shoreline that borders the north side of Long Island Sound. If it's shorebirds like Northern Harriers, Egrets, Plovers, or Sandpipers that you want to see, then we will go find them.

    So, take a look at these resources and if it is a more country type of vacation that you want, there are motels around here that are very clean AND quiet. My email address is on the above list of Connecticut's Birding Pals, I'm Dave Pelletier, I will be your guide.
