Presents Our Moth Index

Welcome to our moth index.These are all moth photos from a couple locations in Connecticut, U.S.A. Please be sure to confirm your findings with other resources! I have attempted to identify these species of moths to the best of my ability (not a pro!) and have received a lot of help from the websites and their people that are listed to the left, on the menu. This moth index is in no way affiliated with, endorsed by, or authenticated by any of these sites. They have all helped this webmaster out a lot. Please visit them, there are professionals out on the internet who are willing to help you identify your moth images.

I only ask that if a mistake is found by anyone, please contact at wingwatchers2 'at' yahoo.com. Just change the 'at' with the @ symbol. This index can always use help with identifications. Some of these photos were contributed by Jodie Bishop and Ron Pelletier, with a few contributions from Bob Patterson the webmaster of The Moth Photographers Group at Mississippi State University.

Please click on a Family name, below, and have a look at what we have collected so far. Most of these specimens are alive when done photographing. The index is here when you need it. These moths are catergorized using the Hodge's system and supplied with common name, if available, plus the scientific name.
