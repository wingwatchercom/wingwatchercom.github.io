  The following poll is related to the use of our natural resources and this question was posed to me, by my 12 year old Granddaughter, Emily. A very good question indeed! Here was her original text:
  "Don't you think that the expanding of populations are destroying wildlife? I do. But I also think that the average American family is doing an even greater job of destroying our world. We enjoy too many luxuries which use up natural resources. Take automobiles for example: the burning of oil contributes to global warming because it makes carbon dioxide. The carbon dioxide is then trapped in the earth's atmospere which captures more thermal energy than it lets out."
