Welcome to a photo journal of nature. Inside are many albums containing photos of birds and insects, wild animals, wild flowers and botanical flowers. See an index of moths that we have collected for the last six years. We hope you will enjoy your visit and will tell your friends about us. Wing Watchers is a family website and we are useful to a few different educational organizations. Our goal is to photograph nature in our state (Connecticut) and show our visitors all the beauty and diversity that Mother Nature gives us if we look for it. Because of human expansion, fracking the Earth for natural gas, and possible climate changes, some or many of these precious species may become endangered or extinct! Also, please visit our Nature Poetry page.
