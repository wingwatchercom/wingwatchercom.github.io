"Ron's Unique Photo Finds"

My Brother, Ron, is an amateur nature photographer. He is always out taking bird and animal photos with his telescope and digital camera. This is called "digiscoping". Anyway, along his many journeys through our state of Connecticut, he also takes photos of unique and weird things like weathervanes, statues, windmills, and anything that is stunning and different. They come in all shapes and sizes and as you can tell by Ron's photos that they are pretty old. People really used to depend on weathervanes to give them the wind directions for farming or navigational reasons.

Ron has given Wing Watchers.net permission to exhibit some of these old but rather beautiful objects that many people never pay a mind to. Well, he does and we thank him for sharing them. To view the photos in this three-way slideshow, just click the buttons at the bottom of the photo for manual. The show may also be automatic, if you choose. Just hit the "start" button. Also, you have the option to view using the drop down menu. Wow! What a script by JavaScript Kit. Enjoy. Hopefully there will be more photos to come.
