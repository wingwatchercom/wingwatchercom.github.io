Connecticut Audubon Center in Fairfield
The Roy and Margot Larsen Wildlife Sanctuary


Habitat:  The Roy and Margot Larson's Wildlife Sanctuary is owned and operated by the Connecticut Audubon Society in Fairfield. There are many disabled birds being taken care of here. On our visit, there were owls, hawks and parakeets being well cared for in their individual homes. There are many marked, wooded trails that wind all through the sanctuary that lead to great birding at field edges, various marshes, and a few ponds in a great package of mixed forest and streams. There is a rather large and beautiful area of smooth, wooded paths that are easily accessable to the handicapped. So there's no excuse, COME SEE! Did we mention that the headquarters building houses a nice little store, exhibits of birds and animals, and public facilities? Enjoy a Sunday out with the family, you can do a lot, here. Please use the map link on our links page to find 2325 Burr St. in Fairfield.
