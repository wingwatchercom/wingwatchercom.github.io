Wing Watchers.com's Directions
To Various Birding Spots

White Memorial Foundation Headquarters, Litchfield-- From Rte. 8, take exit 43 and proceed onto Rte 118w. Follow 118w to the end where it meets Rte.202 (about 5mi.). Here, take a left onto Rte.202w and proceed 2.2mi to Bissel Rd. Take left onto Bissel and take immediate right onto Whitehall Rd.

Point Folley, Bantam Lake, Litchfield-- From Rte 8, take exit 43 and proceed onto Rte.118w. Follow 118w to the end where it meets Rte.202. Here, take a left onto Rte.202w and proceed 2.7mi. to North Shore Dr. Take a left onto North Shore Dr. and follow for about 1mi. to a parking lot on left. Observation platform is in view from here. On the way to here, you crossed a small bridge over the Bantam River that has parking, also.

Little Pond, Litchfield-- From Rte8, take Exit43 and proceed onto Rte.118w. Follow 118w to the end where it meets Rte202. Take a left onto Rte202w and proceed 2.2miles to Bissel Rd. Take a left onto Bissel Rd. and follow to the end. Here, Bissel forks. Take the left fork to end. Take a left onto Whites Woods Rd. The entrance to Little Pond is only about a hundred feet up on the right.

Click here for more directions to many Route 44, Connecticut areas ...

Powder Mill Rd., Canton-- From the junction of Rte.179 and Rte.202, go south on Rte.202 for only 1/8mi. or less and take first right onto Powder Mill Rd. Once here, bear left. This road goes for awhile then turns to a dirt road, following the Farmington River for a couple miles. Also, instead of taking left, go straight. There is a small pond on the right and you can follow along part of Farmington R.

The Collinsville Bridge, Collinsville, Ct.-- From the junction of Rte.179 and Rte.44, head south on 179 for 1 and 1/2mi. where you will cross the bridge over the Farmington R. All down Rte.179, to the bridge is good birding. The only drawback is that there aren't many parking places or pullovers. The Bridge is in town so be aware of where you park.

Roaring Brook Nature Sanctuary, Canton-- To get here, find intersection of Rte.44 and Rte.177s. Directly across, on the north side of 44 is Lawton Rd. Follow Lawton Rd. to the first stop sign. Go straight, staying on Lawton. Follow to the next stop sign. Take a right onto Bahre Corner Rd, and an immediate left (almost straight across) onto Gracey Rd. Follow for about 1/2mi and take a left into the sanctuary.

Fisher Meadows Park, Avon, Ct.-- Find intersection of Rte.44 and Rte.10s. Head south on Rte.10 to the first traffic light and take a right onto Old Farms Rd. Follow for a short while, crossing the Farmington R. Take first right into the park. From I-84, take Exit 39 (Farmington Rte4). Follow Rte.4w to the intersection with Rte10. Here, take a right and follow to next traffic light. Take a left onto Old Farms Rd. Go over the bridge and take first right into park.

Nod Brook Management Area, Simsbury, Ct.-- Find intersection of Rte.44 and Rte.10n. Head north on Rte.10north. Proceed to third traffic light and take a right into the Tower Business Park. Go to stop sign and take a right. Follow to end where this place is. The Farmington River Trail parallels this dirt road. Ponds.

River Road (Rte.181N), New Hartford/Winsted line,Ct.-- From Rte.8N, go to end of highway.This is Winsted. Take a left onto Rte.44E. Follow Rte.44E for 4.2 miles and take a left onto Rte.181N (River Rd.). Right at the beginning of 181N, the road has metal guard rails along the right side of the road. At the end of these guard rails is a dirt road that goes in on the right. Great birding along the Farmington River.

Pleasant Valley Fishing Area, Pleasant Valley, Ct.-- From Rte.8N, go to end of highway.This is Winsted. Take a left onto Rte.44E. Follow Rte.44E for 4.2 miles and take a left onto Rte.181N (River Rd.). Go to the next intersection and take a right. Cross bridge over Farmington River and take immediate right onto a dirt road. When you come to barrier, you can park and walk through some great habitat.

Milford Point, Milford--Take exit 34 off I-95. Go to end of exit and take a left onto Rte.1. At the first light is Landsdale Ave. Take a right here and proceed to traffic light at end of road. Take a right onto Milford Point Rd. Follow this to its end and take right onto Seaview Ave. and follow straight into the parking lot of the Connecticut Audubon Coastal Center.

Silver Sands State Park, Milford--Take exit 35 off I-95. From I-95N, go to end of exit and take a right onto Schoolhouse Rd. and proceed to end of road and junction with Rte. 1. From I-95S, go to end of exit and take a left onto Schoolhouse Rd. and proceed to end. At intersection of Schoolhouse Rd. and Rte1, take a left and proceed on Rte. 1 for about 1/4mi. Take right onto Silver Sands Pkwy. You will come to a traffic light, go straight through and follow into park. There is a large brown sign at beginning of Silver Sands Pkwy.

Caswell Cove and the Mondo Ponds, Milford--Take exit 35 off I-95. From I-95N, go to end of exit and take a left onto Schoolhouse Rd. From I-95S, go to end of exit and take a right onto Schoolhouse Rd. Go to the 2nd traffic light, Naugatuck Ave., and take a right to find the Mondo Ponds about 3/10mi down the road on the right. Now, to find Caswell Cove, go straight at this same light (Naugatuck Ave.)onto Caswell St. Follow to the end and take a left into the Public Access area.

Hammonassett State Park, Madison--Take exit 62 off I-95. If you are on I-95 north, take right at end of exit and follow to end. Park is across the intersection. If you are on I-95 south, take left at end of exit and proceed to end. Park is across the intersection.

Sherwood Island State Park, Fairfield--Take exit 18 off I-95. At end of exit, go south to opening of park.

East Point Beach and Avery Point, Groton--Take exit 87 off I-95. This is Rte.349, the Clarence B. Sharp Highway. Follow to second traffic light and take a right. Proceed to the stop sign at the end and take a left onto East Point Rd.(349s). Follow signs for UConn and Avery Point. Just before Avery Point is Beach Pond Rd. Take a right to reach East Point Beach.

North Cove, Saybrook Point, and South Cove, Old Saybrook--Take exit 67 off I-95. This is the Middlesex Tpk. Follow southward for about 1/2mi and bear left onto Rte154. Follow for about 1mi. and take a left onto North Cove Rd. to get to North Cove. To get to Saybrook Point, stay straight on Rte.154, past the North Cove turn. Rte.154 will take a sharp right. At this spot you have reached Saybrook Point. Continue on Rte.154 and you run right into South Cove.

Places in Stratford--Take exit 30 off I-95. On I-95n this is Rte 113 (Lordship Blvd). On I-95s, at end of exit take a left onto Surf Ave. Proceed to end of Surf Ave. and take a left onto Lordship Blvd. Now that we are all in one place, here are a few good birding spots. Follow Rte.113 North and you will cross through the middle of Great Meadows Marsh. Right after the marsh you will see Sikorsky Memorial Airport on the left. This is the only spot to actually pull over and observe. If you go past the airport and follow Rte.113 for another 3/4mi (approx.), you can take a right onto Oak Bluff Ave. and follow it to Long Beach. If you bypass Oak Bluff Rd, Lordship Blvd. (Rte.113) becomes Stratford Rd. (Rte.113) This whole section is a big loop so follow Rte.113 around and find signs on the right for Short Beach which is on Dorne Dr. Keep going around and Stratford Rd. becomes Main St. (Rte.113). Keep going until you reach the traffic light and take a left onto Access Rd. Here is where Frash Pond is located, right behind the Dunkin' Donuts plaza. If you proceed to the end of Access Rd., take a right onto Lordship Blvd. And head back home to the respective I-95 entrances.

Shippan Point & Cove Park, Stamford--To get to Shippan Point, take Exit 8 off I-95.This is Elm St. I-95s, take a left. I-95n, take a right. Follow Elm St. to Shippan Ave. Stop & Shop is at this intersection. Take a right onto Shippan and follow for 3 lights where you take a left. This is still Shippan Ave. Follow to end. To get to Cove Park, use same directions but go straight at the Stop & Shop intersection. This is now Cove Road. Follow to end and the park.

Griswald Point Park and area, Old Lyme, Ct.--From I-95, take Exit 70 and head out on Rte.156 East.The first right is Ferry Rd. If you take this right, you can visit the Department of Environmental Protection to bird. Keep heading on 156E and you will come to Smith Neck Rd. A right here will bring you to the boat launch on the Connecticut River. Staying on 156E you will come to Old Shore Rd. Take a right, here, and follow until you see two stone pillars (4 or 5 feet tall) on the right hand side of road. Take this right which is White Sands Beech and Griswald Point.

Kellogg Environmental Center, Derby--From Rte.8, take Exit#15. At end of exit, take Rte.34w and follow for 1.5 miles. Take a right onto Lakeview Terrace and follow to end. Take a left into the park.

Osbornedale State Park, Derby--From Rte.8, take Exit#15. At end of exit, take Rte.34w and follow for 1.5 miles. Take a right onto Lakeview Terrace and follow to the end. Take a right onto Hawthorne Ave. and follow to stop sign. Take a left here. Go to next stop sign and take another left onto Chatfield St. This park is directly across from the Derby High School.

Connecticut Audubon Center, Fairfield--From the Merrit Turnpike (Rte.15), take Exit 44. At end of exit, take a left. Follow and take next right onto Rte.58. Follow Rte.58 to the reservoir (on the right). Take a left onto North Street. Follow for about half mile and take a left onto Burr St. The Audubon Center is about a mile down the road, on the right. Big sign at entrance. Also called Larson's Sanctuary.

Station 43 and Vibert Road, South Windsor--From I-91, take Exit35A onto I-291 and follow to Exit4. At end of the exit ramp, take a left onto Rte.5 north. Follow Rte.5n for 1.6 miles and take a left onto Newberry Rd. Follow this to the stop sign at intersection with Main St. The access to Station43 is the dirt road straight across. No vehicles. Parking is tough, few pull offs. Vibert Road can be found by taking a left at the intersection of Newberry & Main St. Go about .4 mile and take a right onto Vibert.

River Road, Kent--From Rte8, take Exit42 and take Rte118W at end of exit. Follow 118W to it's end and take a left onto Rte202W. From here, go 7.4 miles and take a right onto Rte341W. Follow 341W all the way into Kent, 17.2 miles. Go straight at the intersection, remaining on 341W. Cross the bridge over the Housatonic River and take a right onto Skiff Mountain Rd. Follow this for about a mile and take the first right onto River Road. This is a dirt road.
