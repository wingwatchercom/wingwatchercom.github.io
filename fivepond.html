Five Pond Area
Litchfield, CT


Habitat:  The Five Pond Area (as I know it), is a large area of mixed forest with five ponds in it. Fawn Pond, Heron Pond, Teal Pond, Beaver Pond and Plunge Pool attract many interesting species of birds and insects and plant life. The wooded roads within its borders are easily accessible and are marked with various color codes. This is one of the many areas that the White Memorial Foundation owns and cares for. These few pictures aren't enough to display the beauty here. Please use the maps on the bottom of the page (link) to find the intersection of Rte.63 and Rte.61 in Litchfield, Ct. The parking lot, although small, is right here.
