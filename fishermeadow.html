Fisher Meadow Park
Avon, CT


Habitat:  Fisher Meadow Park is a lake area with soccer fields on one side with dirt roads, mixed forest, bordered by the Farmington River and canals. There is plenty of roadside growth and vegetation. This is an excellent and easy access area with handicap facilities.

Location:  Old Farms Road, Avon.
