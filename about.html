The Wing Watchers Crew



  Well, this crew all comes from Connecticut, U.S.A. We are all from the Pelletier family although, through marriage, some last names have changed. The earlier generations of Pelletiers were from Maine and Canada, up in the sticks. I guess that is part of why we all turned out loving nature. Our Mom's side of the family was the Smart family, also from Maine. We came from a fairly large family with seven kids and playing in the woods was the thing to do as we were growing up.
