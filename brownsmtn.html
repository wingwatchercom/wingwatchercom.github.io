Brown's Mountain
Barkhamsted, CT


Habitat:  Brown's Mountain (a.k.a. Ratlum Mountain) is a hilly area with a variety of eco-systems. There are acres of mixed, new and old forest, bordered by Metropolitan District Company land. There are streams and brooks, open fields with brush piles and hedgerows that attract a wide variety of birds, animals and flora. The elevation at the pinnacle is approximately 1100 ft. There are a number of wooded paths that will allow great access to this beautiful mountain.

Location:  Brown's Mountain is private property. If you would like to make an appointment to go birdwatching or observe our many feeders, please email Dave Pelletier.
