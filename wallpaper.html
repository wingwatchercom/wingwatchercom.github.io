There are 5 images behind the 3 boxes that keep sliding through. To see the wallpaper, just click on a sliding thumbnail. To keep the wallpaper, just right click with your mouse and select "set as wallpaper" or "set as background".

In order to use the tiles, below, as a wallpaper you must do a few things, first. After you have right clicked on the picture and saved it as background or wallpaper, these are the steps to follow in Internet Explorer: Click "start" on your taskbar, mouse over the settings catergory and click on your "Control Panel". Find the "Display" folder and click on it. On the first panel that appears, make sure that the background option is set to "Internet Explorer Wallpaper". Right next to that box is a drop down box of options. You will see "Center, Stretch, or Tile". Click on "Tile", click "Apply", click "OK".
