In The Faerie's Garden
[Dedicated to Judy and Dave, who created the garden]

In a beautiful garden green
There sits a little faery queen
She sits upon a lily pad
And sings a song that's very glad

A song of butterflies and birds
A lovely tune without any words
She sees us all with faery eyes
We see her in her faerie guise

What's sitting on the rock over there?
Is it a lovely faery fair?
To us it seems a warty frog
That should be living in a bog

But faeries hide right in plain sight
And only seem themselves at night
So watch for faeries in the sun
They might be froggies having fun.
