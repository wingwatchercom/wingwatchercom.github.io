~NATURE QUIZ~

(Answers at bottom of page.)

  1. What is the difference between a mammal and a reptile?
    a) Reptiles have bigger teeth.
    b) Mammals are warm-blooded.
    c) Mammals have longer tails.
    d) Reptiles live in water.


  2. All insects belong to the animal group known as Arthropods. Do you know what one of the main characteristics of these Arthropods is?
    a) They have eight legs.
    b) Arthropods have gills.
    c) They run fast.
    d) Arthropods have a hard outer shell called an exoskeleton.


  3. How many species of birds do you think the whole world has?
    a) 800 species.
    b) 1,000 species.
    c) 9,000 species.
    d) 1,000,000 species.


  4. Studying the stars is called astronomy. Do you know what the study of plants is called?
    a) Botany
    b) Geology
    c) Anatomy
    d) Astrology
