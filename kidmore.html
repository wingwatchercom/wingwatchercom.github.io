Welcome, Children. This is a place just for you. You will find a neat quiz and a math game, plus a nature story and a Bird Concentration Game in here. You can watch the slide show on this page or you may visit everything on Wing Watchers by clicking on the "Home" button on top. Bring your friends and even your parents to see this site. They might like it, too.

A note to parents: It seems that Kids today are more inquisitive than ever. They want to know everything about everything and with all the new technology out there, they have the opportunity to learn more and become more advanced than earlier generations did.

Computers! The best way to gather lots of info but also a big danger to our young ones. Therefore, we have registered our whole website with the Safe Surf organization. The approved clickable label is on the MAIN PAGE. Please check them out. The Kid's Pages that we have will help children of all ages to learn new things about nature and the environment. Also, our whole website is proper for young people and they should be brought through our whole site. Just click on the mini photos at the top of the page for our photo albums of wild animal, insects, birds, and flowers. Enjoy.
