BIRDING PAL MEMBER

Hi, my name is Dave Pelletier. I'm the guy who tends to this website. I am a registered member of Birding Pal, an international organization with a worldwide database of regular people who love bird watching and are willing to take others to their own local birding spots. Anyone from around the world may therefore be able to connect to a reliable birding source if they plan a vacation or have to be in a certain area for business. They just go to the Birding Pal website, click on the World map, and Voila! A list of local birders plus their email addresses comes up. We Birding Pals are willing to help other birders for the shear fun of it and expect nothing for it, other than that we gain enjoyment through this service. My Resourses To Help You Find Me And Have a Safe and Easy Trip:

For those that want a Birding Pal in other areas of Connecticut, please click on the link above. For the visitors who want to see northwest Connecticut, I'm your guy. I will bring you to some great birding places here and will bring you throughout most of Connecticut to find the birds you want. Below, you will find a link for Bradley International Airport in Windsor Locks, Ct. Within this site you will find plane schedules, prices, car rentals, handicap facilities, and places for lodging. I happen to live only 20 miles from the airport so there would be no problem with meeting you to ensure your piece of mind.

I live on the side of a mountain (Berkshire Foothills) that has seasonal cabins for rent, first come first serve basis. The cabins are all equipped with the basics, frig, running water, stove, and a fireplace for the chilly nights. At the bottom of the mountain is a reservoir that is stocked with Trout and has huge Bass and other sporting fish. If there are no cabins available when you call, don't worry. There are at least 3 good motels in my vicinity. Also, if the weather is warm enough, there is enough room here to just pitch a tent. My landlord will receive any rental payments and I will set that part up for you if you so wish. You will have to arrange your own flight and car rental, and keep me posted by email as to time you will arrive. When you email me, I will give you my telephone number for emergency. I don't want to leave my personal number for the world to see.

ABOUT YOUR GUIDE


My name is Dave, and I would like to guide you to the birds you want to see. I am a 54 year old disabled veteran with a keen love for Nature and the time to go see it. I have a wife, Judy, who helps me in my quest for nature observing and helps keep the website going. I also have two great daughters and three beautiful grandchildren. I have a problem hearing high frequencies such as some birds' sounds, but if they are out there I will spot them. I have been averaging 220 species of birds per year on my Connecticut State List and have about 360 species on my Life List. So, I am available for you anytime you want to visit and I'll guarantee you a good time and maybe add to your Life List.
